The Database Administrator (DBA) is responsible for the physical realization of the database, including  Physical database design and implementation,  Security and integrity control,  Maintenance of the operational system,  Ensuring satisfactory performance of the applications for users.

The role of the DBA is more technically oriented than the role of the DA, requiring detailed knowledge of the target DBMS and the system environment.
